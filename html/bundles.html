Bundle FAQ

The bundle questions we get on the phone.

If yours isn’t here, call 0330 223 66 55 or email us.

Do I have to buy a bundle as-is, or can I customise it?

Customise anything. The six “popular starting points” further down this page are just the combinations we ship most often — use them as a shortcut or change every part before checkout. The step-by-step builder above lets you pick the stand, the screens, and the PC independently.

Can I mix any stand with any computer?

Yes — every stand in the builder works with every computer we sell. We’ll match the graphics card on the PC side to the number of screens you’re driving, automatically. You won’t pick a stand and then discover it needs a specific PC.

What’s included free in every bundle?

Long-length monitor cables (one per screen, cut to the right length for your stand), a Wi-Fi card installed and tested, small USB-powered desk speakers, UK mainland delivery, and the bundle discount (£25–£100 depending on screen count). Every bundle also gets our standard 5-year hardware cover and lifetime UK phone support.

The savings compare to pricing the same items separately from us; compared to piecing it together from other vendors it’s usually more.

How long does it take to build and deliver?

Standard bundle configurations ship in 5–7 working days from order. Custom spec changes (larger RAM, specific GPU, specific storage) add another 2–4 working days. We always tell you up front; we don’t take your money and email you about delays.

Can I add a screen or upgrade the PC later?

Yes. Every bundle PC is built with upgrade paths — extra RAM, additional storage, and — within limits — extra screens via a GPU upgrade. Add-on screens can be ordered separately at any time and we’ll match them to your existing array. Call us with your order number and we’ll pull the exact spec and talk you through what’s sensible.

What if I need help choosing?

Call 0330 223 66 55 during UK hours (Mon–Fri 9am–5:30pm). You’ll speak to Darren or one of the small team who actually build these bundles — no call centre, no sales scripts. Tell us your use case, screen count and budget and we’ll recommend the right bundle. We’ll often talk you out of a spec upgrade you don’t need.
