Can I connect this monitor array to my current PC?

Unfortunately the answer is, it depends. You need a dedicated monitor output on your computer for each monitor you want to connect, so if you want to connect a quad monitor array then you will need four monitor outputs on your existing PC.

The majority of PC's have 1 or 2 monitor outputs on them only. You may be able to upgrade your PC with a graphics card that can support more screens or by adding more graphics cards but this depends on the hardware in your existing PC.

We are more than happy to provide specific advice if you contact us.
