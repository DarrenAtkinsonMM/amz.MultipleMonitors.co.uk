Yes, all of our bundles can be purchased with a customised PC.

You can do this in two ways, simply customise a PC and then add it to the shopping cart then add your preferred stand and screens.

Alternatively, click on Bundles from the top menu and then go through the steps until you have selected your computer, then scroll down to customise before adding the entire bundle to your basket.

There are no differences between the customisation options on an individual PC or a Bundle PC.
