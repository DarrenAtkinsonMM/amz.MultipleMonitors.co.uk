What type of monitors does this computer support?

This computer comes with two graphics cards installed which can each support two screens, giving you the option of connecting up to four separate monitors to this PC.

There will be two VGA (analogue) monitor outputs and two DVI (digital) ones. We will also supply two DVI to VGA adapters with the computer at no extra cost, this will allow you to support a total of four VGA connected screens.

Monitors can be configured in clone or extended desktop mode.
