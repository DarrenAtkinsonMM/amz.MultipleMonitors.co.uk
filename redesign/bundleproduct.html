Bundle & PC questions

The questions we get at this decision point.

Specific to this bundle and this machine — not generic PC-shop answers. Got one not listed? Call us on 0330 223 66 55.

Can I swap the stand or screens in this bundle?

Yes — nothing is locked in until you complete checkout. Use the “Change” link on any of the three chips at the top of the page to go back into the builder with your current picks preserved. Swap the stand size, change screen model, even change PC — the bundle discount and the free extras will recalculate automatically.

If you just want to keep this bundle but tune the PC spec, use the configurator above — the bundle total updates live with every choice.

Can I upgrade the CPU, RAM or storage later?

Yes — every Trader PC is designed for upgrades. The motherboard supports every 14th-gen Intel CPU option we sell (up to the i9 14900KF), so you can start with the i5 14400F now and upgrade later without changing the board.

RAM is straightforward — two DIMM slots, up to 64 GB DDR4 3200. Storage adds the same way: a second M.2 slot and 4 SATA ports for extra SSDs or HDDs. We’ll walk you through any upgrade on the phone — usually a 15-minute job.

Does Windows come pre-installed and activated?

Yes. Every machine ships with Windows 11 Home fully installed, activated, and tuned for trading workloads — Windows Defender exclusions for your platforms, telemetry minimised, power plan set to high-performance, scheduled updates set for out-of-hours. DisplayFusion is also pre-installed and licensed.

Windows 11 Pro is available at +£45 if you need BitLocker, Remote Desktop host, or domain join.

What exactly arrives in the bundle delivery?

Your Trader PC (configured as above), three Acer 24” monitors, the Triple Horizontal Synergy Stand with all mount plates and assembly hardware, three premium 3 m DisplayPort cables, a Wi-Fi / Bluetooth card (fitted), a pair of desktop speakers, a UK power lead, a printed setup guide, and a recovery USB drive.

Everything in one delivery. If you’re missing so much as a screw, call us and we’ll courier it next day.

How do I connect my existing monitors if I swap them in?

The standard nVidia RTX A400 has four mini-DisplayPort outputs and ships with four mini-DP to DisplayPort adapters. If your monitors have HDMI only, we can include DP-to-HDMI cables at cost — just call and ask before you check out.

If you’re running more than four screens or monitors with unusual inputs, tell us what you have when you order and we’ll ship with the right cables in the box.

What happens if something fails under warranty?

In year one on the PC, we come to you (OnSite) or collect and repair — at our discretion, usually depending on what’s failed. Years two to five are collection or return-to-base. You can extend OnSite to year two (+£75) or year three (+£150) at checkout.

Stand: multi-year warranty (frame is typically covered for life). Screens: manufacturer warranty, typically 3 years. Any failed component across the whole bundle — one number to call.

How do I move my trading software and data across from my old PC?

We do this for free as part of lifetime support. Phone or email us the day your bundle arrives — we’ll remote-connect (TeamViewer or similar), help you install MT4/MT5/NinjaTrader/TradingView, migrate your templates, indicators, EAs and chart layouts, and get your broker connections back up. Usually takes 45–90 minutes depending on how much you’re moving.
