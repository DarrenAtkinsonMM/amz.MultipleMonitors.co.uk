Will my monitors fit?

Check if your screens, fit on this stand.

Every Synergy Stand is designed to use the two VESA patterns found on most modern monitors. Check the pattern on the back of your monitor and confirm its dimensions sit within the envelope shown below.

Verified compatible with every monitor sold in our bundles and arrays.

VESA compatibility

Both patterns, every mount
75 × 75
VESA MIS-D, 75
100 × 100
VESA MIS-D, 100

If you already have screens or are purchasing some new ones then look for a VESA 75 or VESA 100 rating, sometimes described as a 'Wall mount interface'.

This is the four screw holes in the back of a screen in a square configuration. If your monitor has them then it should be compatible with this Synergy Stand.
