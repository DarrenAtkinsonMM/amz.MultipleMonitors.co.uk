Common trader questions

The questions we get on the phone, answered.

18 years of pre-sale conversations has given us a solid list. If yours isn’t here, get in touch.

Do I really need a specialist trading computer?

Honest answer: if you’re running one or two screens on MT4 or Trading View, a decent off-the-shelf PC will get you going. The case for a specialist machine kicks in when you start needing more screens or are running multiple platforms. If you need four or more screens, run more than one trading app simultaneously, run overnight backtesting, run Bloomberg, Trading Technologies, Reuters, or when any millisecond of chart lag starts costing you.

If we think you don’t need us, we’ll tell you. That’s why our Trustpilot score looks like it does.

Can I use a gaming PC for a trading workload?

Good gaming PCs are built for one specific task, gaming. Gaming performance is pretty much all driven by the power of the graphics card whereas most trading platforms use little graphics power in comparison, they rely more on the raw CPU speed.

Often you find cheaper 'gaming' computers are actually pretty low end in terms of CPU and RAM and someone has just stuck a mid-range gaming graphics card in it and called it a gaming PC. This is not optimal for trading workloads and whilst it 'may' do the job with some trading platforms, you're still paying a significant amount of money for something not particularly suited to the job at hand.

What’s the real difference between the Trader PC and the Trader Pro?

From a purely technical point of view the Trader PC uses the slightly older (but still new components) Intel 14th generation processors combined with DDR4 RAM. The Trader Pro PC uses Intel's latest Core Ultra Platform combined with DDR5 RAM.

The Trader PC is the right call for traders running MT4/MT5, TradingView, MultiCharts, or NinjaTrader without heavy overnight strategy analysis. It is great for Full HD screen setups. Single-thread performance is key for these platforms and the Trader PC is very good there.

The Trader Pro is worth it if you're running more intensive platforms like Bloomberg, working in NinjaTrader 8 or TradeStation with multi-instrument backtests, or running a lot of platforms simultaneously. The extra speed and multi-tasking capabilities of the Core Ultra processors combined with extra RAM and more powerful graphics setups make for a very solid upgrade over the Trader PC.

Someone told me I need a powerful graphics card to run multiple screens, is that right?

Not really, but it does depend on the number and resolution of your screens to a certain extent.

One requirement of a graphics setup is having the right number of monitor ports available, if you want to run 6 screens then you need 6 monitor output ports, this usually means having 2 graphics cards installed.

The next consideration is the resolution of your screens. Higher resolution displays are more demanding than lower ones. Running four 4K screens is the equivalent of powering 16 normal Full HD monitors which is obviously a lot and it puts a lot more strain on your graphics setup.

We spend a lot of time discussing these points with customers but as a generalisation, if you are running Full HD screens (1920 x 1080 resolution) then any of the standard lower priced graphics setups we offer are more than capable. They will all handle up to four QHD screens easily as well. If you are going to a higher resolution than QHD or want to run 6 or more of them then upgrading to more powerful graphics setups may be worth exploring.

Can I upgrade later?

It depends on the specific upgrade but the answer is usually yes. We use premium components that generally have spare capacity for future upgrades to things like RAM, hard drives and graphics cards.

CPU / processor upgrades can be more convoluted as often you will need to also swap the motherboard and potentially the cooling system too. This depends on whether Intel or AMD require a new motherboard for the new CPU, sometimes they do, othertimes a motherboard will support multiple CPU releases.

If you are looking to buy something now with a view to future upgrades just let us know. Because we build everything up from scratch we can accommodate pretty much anything you are thinking.
