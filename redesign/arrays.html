Array questions

The questions we get before ordering, answered.

17 years of array builds has given us a solid list. If yours isn’t here, call us on 0330 223 66 55.

Are you sure these screens will fit together properly?

Yes — that’s the whole point of buying an array from us rather than sourcing parts separately. Every stand and every screen we sell has been checked for VESA compatibility (75×75 or 100×100), bezel thickness, depth clearance on the mount, and the overall footprint on a standard desk.

Before your array ships it’s physically assembled in the workshop with the exact screens you’ve picked, so any tolerance issues get caught before the box leaves us — not after you’ve spent an hour unboxing.

How are the screens mounted to the stand?

Standard VESA arms on the Synergy stand. Every screen we sell has either a 75×75 or 100×100 VESA pattern on the back, and our arms take both without an adapter plate. The stand itself clamps to the desk or slots into a grommet hole — no holes drilled, no desk damage, and it can come off again later without leaving a mark.

What if my desk can’t take a clamp?

We have desk clamp, grommet-mount, and freestanding base options for every Synergy stand. If your desk is glass, hollow, or has an awkward edge, tell us at order and we’ll spec the right base — no extra charge. A few customers have quirky setups (corner desks, standing desks, motorised lifts); call us and we’ll talk it through before you order.

What cables and accessories come with an array?

A 3-metre premium video cable for every screen (DisplayPort or HDMI to match your PC’s outputs), cut long enough to reach a floor-standing tower without tension. Every array also gets a power extension strip sized to the number of screens, so you’re not chasing a single 4-way and a sea of adapters.

We don’t bundle pointless extras you won’t use — no no-name speakers, no cheap USB hubs. If you want those, pick them up on the add-ons page.

How does an array actually arrive?

One delivery. The stand ships flat-packed with an illustrated step-by-step guide (10–15 minutes to assemble with a hex key we include). The screens ship in their own boxes, fully VESA-ready. Cables and power strip come in a labelled bag so nothing gets lost in the packing foam. Typical lead time is 3–5 working days from order.

Can I add a computer to this later?

Absolutely — you can buy a Trader PC, Ultra PC, or Extreme PC from us at any time, and we’ll match the graphics card to the number of screens on your array. If you already know you want a PC too, our Bundles page combines the array with a PC at a further £100+ discount on top of the array savings.

What’s the warranty situation?

Synergy stands carry a lifetime warranty on the frame (we build them to outlast the screens that go on them). Screens are covered by the manufacturer’s warranty, typically 3 years. Cables and power extensions are covered by us for 12 months. And if an array arrives and the fit isn’t right — wrong size, wrong orientation, anything — our 30-day money-back guarantee covers the full set, no paperwork dance.
