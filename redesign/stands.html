Built for today’s bigger screens

Supports up to 28″ screens — with room to curve.

Screens keep getting larger. 24″ & 27″ widescreens are now our most popular sizes, and most customers now like to go bigger. We designed the Synergy Stand knowing that trend wasn’t going away.

Many competitor stands specify ‘up to 24″’ — which leaves no room to angle the outer screens inward for a proper curved layout. Every Synergy Stand is designed to comfortably mount monitors up to and including 28″ widescreens, and still achieve a gentle curve at full screen count.
