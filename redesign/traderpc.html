Trader PC questions

The six questions we get most often.

Specific to this machine — not generic PC-shop answers. Got a question not listed? Call us on 0330 223 66 55.

Can I upgrade the CPU, RAM or storage later?

Yes — every Trader PC is designed for upgrades. The motherboard supports every 14th-gen Intel CPU option we sell (up to the i9 14900KF), so you can start with the i5 14400F now and upgrade later without changing the board.

RAM is straightforward — two DIMM slots, up to 64 GB DDR4 3200. Storage adds the same way: a second M.2 slot and 4 SATA ports for extra SSDs or HDDs. We’ll walk you through any upgrade on the phone — usually a 15-minute job.

Does Windows come pre-installed and activated?

Yes. Every machine ships with Windows 11 Home fully installed, activated, and tuned for trading workloads — Windows Defender exclusions for your platforms, telemetry minimised, power plan set to high-performance, scheduled updates set for out-of-hours. DisplayFusion is also pre-installed and licensed.

Windows 11 Pro is available at +£45 if you need BitLocker, Remote Desktop host, or domain join.

What’s included in the box?

The PC itself, a UK power lead, a printed setup guide, and a recovery USB drive for Windows reinstalls. Anything you add in the configurator (keyboard, speakers, Wi-Fi card, etc.) arrives in the same carton, bench-tested with the machine.

What’s not in the box: monitors and cables. If you need those, a trader bundle is typically £100–£200 cheaper than buying separately.

How do I connect my existing monitors?

The standard nVidia RTX A400 has four mini-DisplayPort outputs and ships with four mini-DP to DisplayPort adapters. If your monitors have HDMI only, we can throw in DP-to-HDMI cables at cost — just call and ask.

If you’re running more than four screens or monitors with unusual inputs, tell us what you have when you order and we’ll ship with the right cables in the box.

What happens if a part fails under warranty?

In year one, we come to you (OnSite) or collect and repair — at our discretion, usually depending on what’s failed. Years two to five are collection or return-to-base. You can extend OnSite to year two (+£75) or year three (+£150) at checkout.

In practice: if a trader’s machine goes down during market hours, we’ll often courier a replacement part the same day and recover yours at leisure. Not a written policy — just what we do because we’d want someone to do it for us.

How do I move my trading software and data across from my old PC?

We do this for free as part of lifetime support. Phone or email us the day your new machine arrives — we’ll remote-connect (TeamViewer or similar), help you install MT4/MT5/NinjaTrader/TradingView, migrate your templates, indicators, EAs and chart layouts, and get your broker connections back up. Usually takes 45–90 minutes depending on how much you’re moving.

Question not on the list?

Seventeen years of pre-sale conversations means we’ve heard most things. Phone or email Darren — he’ll give you a straight answer, or tell you honestly if the Trader PC isn’t the right machine for you.
