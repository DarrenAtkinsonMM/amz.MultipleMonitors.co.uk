Bundle questions

The questions we get before ordering, answered.

17 years of bundle conversations has given us a solid list. If yours isn’t here, call us on 0330 223 66 55.

Do all your stands work with all your computers?

Yes — every Synergy stand we sell is compatible with every PC in our range. Pick whichever order you like: choose the stand for the screen layout you want, and we’ll match the graphics card on the PC side to drive the right number of screens. You can’t paint yourself into a corner.

What’s actually included for free in a bundle?

Every bundle ships with: a long-length high-quality video cable for every screen (3m DisplayPort or HDMI as needed), a wifi card, a pair of desktop speakers, and free UK mainland delivery. On top of that the bundle discount kicks in automatically — typically £50–£100 off depending on size.

On a 6-screen bundle this works out to around £270 of included value vs. sourcing the same parts separately.

How long does a bundle take to build and deliver?

Standard bundles ship in 5–7 working days from order. Custom specs (bigger RAM, specific GPU, specific storage layout) add another 2–4 working days. We always tell you up front at order — we don’t take your money and email you about delays.

Everything arrives in one delivery, so you’re not chasing boxes from three different suppliers.

Can I change a bundle after I’ve picked one of your starting points?

Absolutely. The popular starting points are just that — starting points. You can swap the stand, change screen size, pick a different PC, bump the RAM, switch the CPU, anything. Nothing is locked in until you complete checkout.

Can I save my bundle and share it with someone else?

Yes. Every configured bundle has its own shareable URL — use the “Copy link to this bundle” button in the running summary on the right. Useful if you want to discuss it with a business partner, get sign-off, or come back to it later.

What does the warranty cover?

Every PC in a bundle is covered by our 5-year hardware warranty and lifetime UK phone support. The Synergy stand carries a separate multi-year warranty (usually lifetime on the frame), and the screens are covered by the manufacturer’s warranty (typically 3 years). The full breakdown is shown on every configured bundle before checkout.

Can I return a bundle if it’s not right?

30-day money-back guarantee on the whole bundle. If it arrives and isn’t right — wrong size, wrong layout, whatever — we collect it, refund you, no paperwork dance.
