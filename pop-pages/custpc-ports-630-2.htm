This graphics setup uses a powerful and silent nVidia GT 1030 2GB card alongside a capable GT 710.

Monitor Ports

The configuration has 2 DVI-D, 2 HDMI and 1 VGA ports, you can use up to 4 of these simultaneously to support a maximum of up to 4 monitors.

We also supply two handy DVI to HDMI converters which allow support for either 4 DVI or 4 HDMI monitors from your PC.

Resolutions Supported

Each digital monitor port can support a maximum screen resolution of 2560 x 1600.

Noise Levels

The cards are passively cooled which means they are completely silent.
