Bootable Backup Hard Drive

For total peace of mind can choose an amazing bootable backup drive solution which will save you time and money in the event of various computer issues.

Every day, week or month a complete copy of your main 'C drive' is taken and stored on a completely separate internal hard drive. If your main C drive fails at any time you can simply boot from this backup drive and be instantly back up and running.
