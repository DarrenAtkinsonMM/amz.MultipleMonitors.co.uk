Acronis True Image 2010

Acronis True Image is an industry leading and award winning backup and restoration solution for desktop computers.

Preinstalled on each Multiple Monitors PC, an Acronis True Image backup is created containing your full operating system, all drivers and multi screen settings. You can then restore this backup at any time to reset your machine to its initial state.

You can also make backup images of your own programs and files for future restoration any time you wish.
