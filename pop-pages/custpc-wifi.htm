Adding a wireless network card to your new computer lets it detect and join wireless networks for network and Internet access.

About Network Connections

All of our computers feature an ethernet (LAN) port, these accept standard RJ45 network cables for Internet and network access.

Our standard WiFi card option uses the fastest possible Wireless AX protocol which means you get the best speed that you can. The cards will work at lower speeds if your router does not support the faster protocols.

As a bonus all of our WiFi cards now come with built in Bluetooth functionality allowing for connection of a wide range of accessories.
