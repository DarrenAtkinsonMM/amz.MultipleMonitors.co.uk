This graphics setup uses a powerful and silent nVidia GT 1030 2GB card alongside two capable GT 710's.

Monitor Ports

The configuration has 3 DVI-D, 3 HDMI and 2 VGA ports, you can use up to 6 of these simultaneously to support a maximum of up to 6 monitors.

We also supply three handy DVI to HDMI converters which allow support for either 6 DVI or 6 HDMI monitors from your PC.

Resolutions Supported

Each digital monitor port can support a maximum screen resolution of 2560 x 1600.

Noise Levels

The cards are passively cooled which means they are completely silent.
