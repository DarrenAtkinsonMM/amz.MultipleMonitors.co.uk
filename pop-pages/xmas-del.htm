Christmas Delivery Information

To guarantee a pre-Christmas delivery from Multiple Monitors we have an order cut-off date of Friday 16th December @ 5:00PM.

Orders up to this time will be built, tested and dispatched in time for Christmas. Orders received after this will still be processed however due to courier schedules will not be dispatched until after the Christmas break.

We wish all our customers a very Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year.
