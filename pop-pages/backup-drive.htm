Backup & Restore - Software & Hard Drive

For the ultimate in backup and restore, this computer comes with the award winning Acronis True Image software and a dedicated backup hard drive.

Acronis is installed and configured when your computer is first built, a full backup image is created and stored on a secondary hard drive allowing you to restore the PC to its initial state whenever you may need to, even if you primary drive fails!

You can also make backup images of your own programs and files for future restoration any time you wish.
