This graphics setup uses four dual screen capable nVidia GT 210 1GB graphics cards.

Monitor Ports

The setup has 4 DVI-I, 4 HDMI and 4 VGA ports, you can use up to 8 of these simultaneously to support a maximum of 8 monitors.

We also supply 4 DVI - VGA adapters free of charge to allow you to connect VGA screens up to the DVI ports to enable 8 x VGA monitors.

Resolutions Supported

Each monitor port can support a maximum screen resolution of 2560 x 1600.

Noise Levels

The cards are passively cooled which means they are completely silent.
