This graphics setup uses four powerful and silent nVidia GT 730 2GB dual monitor graphics cards.

Monitor Ports

The configuration has 4 DVI-D, 4 HDMI and 4 VGA ports, you can use up to 8 of these simultaneously to support a maximum of 8 monitors.

We also supply four handy DVI to HDMI converters which allow support for either 8 DVI or 8 HDMI monitors from your PC.

Resolutions Supported

Each monitor port can support a maximum screen resolution of 2560 x 1600.

Noise Levels

The cards are passively cooled which means they are completely silent.
