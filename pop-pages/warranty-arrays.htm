Array Warranty Information

All Multiple Monitor arrays are backed with a free 30 day collection and fix policy which means any initial problems will be dealt with at our full expense.

All TFT screens and stands are supplied with full manufacturers warranties. TFT panels usually have a 3 year warranty package whilst the stands come with between 3 and 5 year warranties depending on the original manufacturer.

We are happy to provide technical support and advice for any array issues and potential warranty claims.
