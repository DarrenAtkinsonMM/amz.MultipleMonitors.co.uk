Traditionally if you wanted to hear anything more than the odd beep from your computer then you needed to purchase an additional sound card, this was responsible for creating any audio and connecting speakers.

Now a days pretty much every motherboard produced has a decent sound card built into it. Only people with specific requirements would normally consider adding in an extra sound card. (We can supply and install these into new PC's if requested).

Just Add Speakers

So on the back of every computer we sell you will have standard audio jack inputs and outputs, these will connect to any 3.5mm audio cable found on pretty much any speakers, headphones or microphones.

We also offer a set of Logitech desktop speakers as an optional upgrade if required for your new PC.

These offer surprisingly good sound from a small package and are powered via a spare USB port, this means that they don't take up an electrical plug socket which is useful.
