The following rules apply to all orders placed:

UK Customers

UK VAT is applied to all orders.

EU Customers

UK VAT is not charged by us for all sales to both businesses and private individuals, however a VAT / import duty charge will be issued to you by the couriers when the items are shipped and clear customs in your country.

The amount charged should be equal to your local VAT rate.

VAT registered businesses should be able to claim this back via their standard accounting procedures however you should check with your accountant / local custom offices if you are unsure on this.

For business customers we will require both your VAT number and EORI number for the customs documents. For private individuals a PID or personal identification number is also required for some countries.

We will email you when processing your order for these details.

Non-EU Customers

UK VAT is removed before payment.

Non EU customers may be liable for a VAT or Import Duty charge when items arrive in your country, the couriers or your local customs office will contact you for this before final delivery takes place.

Please check with your local customs office for further details on any charges applicable.

Notes For All Non UK Sales

Any VAT, import duty, or tarrif charges due from your local customs office are your respnsibility to pay, we have no liability for them.

Failure to pay them will see the items ordered returned to us at which point we will issue a refund minus any shipping costs we have incurred.
