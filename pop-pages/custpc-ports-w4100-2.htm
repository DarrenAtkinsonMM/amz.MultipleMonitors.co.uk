This graphics setup uses two quad monitor capable AMD W4100 2GB graphics cards.

Monitor Ports

The setup gives you 8 Mini DisplayPort graphics outputs on your new PC, they supply a digital signal to your monitors.

To connect to DVI or HDMI screens you will need to purchase a Mini DisplayPort to DVI or HDMI adapter for each screen you want to connect.

Resolutions Supported

Each monitor port can support a maximum screen resolution of 2560 x 1600 with a dual link DVI adapter or 1920 x 1200 with a single-link DVI adapter.

Noise Levels

The cards carry a low noise cooling fan so they are quiet but not silent.
