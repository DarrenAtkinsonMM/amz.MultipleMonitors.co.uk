This graphics setup uses the Intel UHD graphics capabilities of your CPU to support your monitors..

Monitor Ports

The setup has 1 DisplayPort and 1 DVI-D graphics outputs on your new PC, they supply a digital signal to your monitors.

Resolutions Supported

The DisplayPort output can support a maximum screen resolution of 3840 x 2160 at 60Hz. You can also support a second monitor via the DVI port at a max resolution of 1920 x 1080. Both ports are capable of supporting lower graphics resolutions so one QHD could be run from the DisplayPort.

Noise Levels

The graphics ports are built directly into the motherboard and are completely noiseless.
