Free Cables & Connection Guarantee Promise

To connect any monitor to any computer you need a video cable, to create the connection between PC and screen. The problem is which cable is right for your setup?

Monitors can have four different types of inputs on them, VGA, DVI, HDMI or DisplayPort, these are all different and require different cables.

From the PC side of things graphics cards usually have a mix of outputs, VGA, DVI-D, DVI-I, HDMI, Mini-HDMI, DisplayPort & Mini-DisplayPort are all commonly found.

Some connections are analogue, some are digital (some can be both!), some can be converted into another connection type, some can't, and they all pretty much support different maximum resolutions which dictates what size screens can be used.

As you can probably tell, cables can be a nightmare.
