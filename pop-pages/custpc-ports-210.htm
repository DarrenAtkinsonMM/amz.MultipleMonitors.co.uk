This graphics setup uses one graphics card, an Intel 650 integrated GPU.

Monitor Ports

The setup has 1 DVI-I, 1 HDMI and 1 VGA port, you can use up to 2 of these simultaneously to support a maximum of 2 monitors.

We also supply 1 DVI - HDMI adapter free of charge to allow you to connect two DVI or HDMI screens up to this computer.

Resolutions Supported

Each monitor port can support a maximum screen resolution of 2560 x 1600.

Noise Levels

The cards are passively cooled which means they are completely silent.
