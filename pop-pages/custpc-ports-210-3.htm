This graphics setup uses three dual screen capable nVidia GT 710 1GB graphics cards.

Monitor Ports

The setup has 3 DVI-I, 3 HDMI and 3 VGA ports, you can use up to 6 of these simultaneously to support a maximum of 6 monitors.

We also supply 3 DVI - HDMI adapters free of charge to allow you to connect six DVI or HDMI screens up to this computer.

Resolutions Supported

Each monitor port can support a maximum screen resolution of 2560 x 1600.

Noise Levels

The cards are passively cooled which means they are completely silent.
